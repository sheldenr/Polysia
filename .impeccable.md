## Design Context

### Users
Polysia Learning Mode primarily serves intermediate Mandarin learners doing daily, self-directed structured practice. They use this page to move smoothly between complementary study modes in a single visit.

### Brand Personality
Calm, premium, and focused. The interface should feel trustworthy and polished, reducing friction while keeping momentum.

### Aesthetic Direction
Default to light theme. Use refined spacing, clear hierarchy, and restrained accents that support a premium study experience without visual noise.

### Design Principles
1. Make balance obvious: all three learning modes should carry equal visual weight and clear entry points.
2. Minimize cognitive load: prioritize legible content, calm contrast, and clear next actions.
3. Preserve momentum: optimize for quick transitions between modes without losing orientation.
4. Reward consistency subtly: surface progress and streaks as supportive context, not the main focus.
5. Keep interactions deliberate: motion and emphasis should communicate state, never distract.
